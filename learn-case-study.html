Case Study: Real‑World Home Automations

Based on a public walkthrough of Jimmy Hawkins’ home. Devices and automations organized by room and mapped to our packages.

Living Room

  • Devices: Leviton scene controller, presence sensor (mmWave), Govee M1 LED strips (soldered across shelves), Eve Flare portable lamp (Thread/HomeKit).
  • Automations: TV scene dims lights; presence keeps lights on while seated; LED scenes for sports and alarm state.
Packages: AlexaGoogleHome Assistant

Primary Bedroom

  • Devices: Hunter Wi‑Fi fan (HomeKit), Aqara motion, SwitchBot Curtain (gen1) + solar, SmartThings Station (Qi charger + routine trigger).
  • Automations: Night routine on phone‑dock; curtains AM/PM; fan tied to presence/night scenes.
Packages: AlexaGoogleHome Assistant

Primary Bathroom

  • Devices: Yoolax Zigbee shades, 4‑in‑1 humidity sensor, Zooz scene button, smart outlet for hair tools, Echo Flex, Oral‑B BT toothbrush.
  • Automations: Shades guarded while showering (humidity); button colors command shades; outlet cuts power on away; toothbrush triggers night shutdown.
Packages: Home AssistantAlexa

Kitchen

  • Devices: U by Moen faucet; Sharp microwave drawer (Wi‑Fi); Echo Show 15 (Fire TV); Zooz Z‑Wave dimmers + association; Aqara temp/air sensor near coffee maker.
  • Automations: Voice preset fills coffee pot; circuits dim together via association; coffee steam spike announces ready.
Packages: AlexaHome Assistant

Laundry Room

  • Devices: Energy‑monitoring smart plug (washer), vibration sensor (dryer), water leak sensor tied to main shutoff.
  • Automations: Alerts when cycles finish; automatic main shutoff on leak.
Packages: Home Assistant

Kids’ Rooms

Son

  • Govee Glide Hexa panels; IKEA Zigbee shade; Third Reality button for bedside lamp.
  • Night scene; shade open AM/close PM; manual bedside control.

Daughter

  • SwitchBot Curtain (latest) via SwitchBot Matter Hub; Govee table lamp; “Fandelier” via Bond/Broadlink RF bridge.
  • Curtains scheduled; voice/automation control for RF fan.
Packages: AlexaGoogleHome Assistant

Entry / Mesh Strategy

  • Devices: Mix of Jasco/Enbrighten Zigbee & Z‑Wave dimmers (blue LED match).
  • Practice: At least one Zigbee and one Z‑Wave device per room to strengthen meshes.
Packages: Home Assistant

Pet Automation

  • Devices: Vibration sensor on dog’s bell.
  • Automation: Whole‑home announcement + phone/watch notifications when bell rings.
Packages: Home AssistantAlexaGoogle

Garage

  • Devices: MyQ opener (Amazon Key), Dome Z‑Wave “smart mousetrap”.
  • Automations: Delivery access; instant alert when mousetrap triggers.
Packages: AlexaHome Assistant

Office

  • Devices: Leviton voice dimmer (Alexa‑built‑in), Zigbee dimmer, smart‑plugged space heater; Wyze cam with barking alerts; Nest Hub + HomePod minis; Third Reality Zigbee blinds; Dreametech L20 Ultra vac/mop.
  • Automations: Heater cuts off on away; blinds half‑drop on high lux; nightly vacuum with headlight for object detection.
Packages: AlexaGoogleHome Assistant

Controller Platform

  • Home now primarily runs on Home Assistant Yellow for flexible local automations; SmartThings still used for testing.
