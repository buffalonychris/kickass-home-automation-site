Which system is right for you?

Alexa‑Based

  • Best for simple voice routines
  • Leviton voice dimmers; Ring/Key
  • Works with SwitchBot via Matter bridge

Google‑Based

  • Strong media + displays
  • Best with Android households
  • Good with Matter bridges (SwitchBot, etc.)

Home Assistant

  • Local control; advanced rules
  • Z‑Wave + Zigbee mesh
  • Expose to Alexa/Google for voice
