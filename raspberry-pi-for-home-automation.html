Is a Raspberry Pi good for home automation?

A Pi is great for learning and light‑duty control. For bigger homes or long‑term reliability, consider purpose‑built hardware.

Good fits

  • Small setups, labs, and tinkering
  • Home Assistant OS with backups
  • Non‑critical automations

Consider alternatives

  • Dozens of Zigbee/Z‑Wave devices (mesh + CPU/IO)
  • Mission‑critical automations (locks, water shutoff)
  • 24/7 reliability without SD‑card wear

We’ll spec hardware based on device count, protocols, and uptime requirements.
